Assessment of

Building Permit  Drawing

This checklist is developed to evaluate building design and drawings submitted in the Municipality according to the codal provisions. Additionally, it helps to prepare an inventory of building practices and analysis for Building Code Compliance. This assessment is being carried out by the NAtional Society for Earthquake Technology-Nepal (NSET) together with Municiplaity.
